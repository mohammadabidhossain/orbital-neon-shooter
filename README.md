# Orbital Neon Shooter: Redux

A fast-paced, retro-style 3D orbital shooter game built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript using the three.js library.

## About the Game

In Orbital Neon Shooter: Redux, you are the last line of defense for a star system under siege. Pilot your advanced starfighter in a perilous orbit around a sun, fending off waves of relentless alien attackers. The minimalist neon visuals and fast-paced action will keep you on the edge of your seat.

## How to Play

The goal of the game is to survive as long as possible and achieve the highest score by destroying enemy ships.

### Controls

-   **WASD**: Adjust your ship's orbit. Use 'W' and 'S' to move your orbit closer or further from the sun, and 'A' and 'D' to change your orbital direction.
-   **Mouse**: Aim your ship and click to shoot.

### Gameplay

-   **Enemies**: Hostile ships will continuously spawn and home in on your position. Destroy them to score points.
-   **The Sun**: Be careful not to get too close to the sun! Your ship will overheat and eventually be destroyed.
-   **Power-ups**:
    -   **Rapid Fire (Yellow Box)**: Temporarily increases your ship's firing rate.
    -   **Shield (Blue Box)**: Protects you from a single enemy collision.

## Technologies Used

-   **HTML5**: For the game's structure.
-   **CSS3 & TailwindCSS**: For styling the UI elements.
-   **JavaScript**: For the game logic.
-   **three.js**: For 3D rendering.
